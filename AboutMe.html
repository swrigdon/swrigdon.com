A Bit of My Background

I am a 20 year old native Floridian. Since I was in diapers, I've had a deep passion for video games. Since I was 13, I've been teaching myself programming, and I've spent the past year or so delving into small amounts of game development.

My Schooling

During my junior and senior years of high school, I dual-enrolled at Lake-Sumter State College, maintaining a 3.2 GPA while maintaining my 3.9 high school GPA. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor's of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Central Florida, where I hold a 3.1 overall GPA.

My Goals

My ultimate goal is to earn a spot within a game development studio and contribute to making games that I can be proud of, and that are fun for everyone to play. In the meantime, I am hoping to learn as much as possible about professional software development and how to produce real-world projects.
